Rotisserie Chicken

Description

Prepare to enjoy the mouthwatering flavors of perfectly cooked rotisserie chicken with this simple and delicious recipe. With a few key ingredients and some patience, you can achieve a golden-brown, crispy skin and juicy, tender meat that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're hosing a family dinner or looking for a versatile meal prep option, this rotisserie chicken will be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 1/4 Tbsp Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Paprika

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat an outdoor grill for a high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Season chicken cavity with a pinch of salt. Tie legs together with kitchen string; then tie wings to the bird. Secure chicken on a rotisserie attachment.
  3. Place rotisserie over the preheated grill and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, quickly mix together butter, 1 tablespoon of salt, paprika, and pepper. Turn the grill down to medium and baste chicken with butter mixture.
  5. Close the lid and cook over medium heat, basting occasionally, until chicken is cooked through and internal temperature reaches 180 degrees F (83 degrees C), 1 to 1.5 hours.
  6. Remove chicken from the rotisserie and let rest for 10 to 15 minutes before carving.
