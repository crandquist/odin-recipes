Rotisserie Chicken

Description

Prepare to enjoy the mouthwatering flavors of perfectly cooked rotisserie chicken with this simple and delicious recipe. With a few key ingredients and some patience, you can achieve a golden-brown, crispy skin and juicy, tender meat that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're hosing a family dinner or looking for a versatile meal prep option, this rotisserie chicken will be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1 Tbsp Salt
  • 1/4 Tbsp Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Paprika
